OUR DEFINITION

What We Mean by "Landscape"

We take a broad view â€” here's the Group's working definition:

The Subject

The photographic portrayal of all elements of the land, sea and sky, whether natural or built, or influenced by human endeavour.

The Scope

Mountains, hills, farmland, coasts, bodies of water, forests, populated areas, and industrial landscapes.

The Technique

Traditional or other techniques including infrared, multiple exposures, ICM, abstraction, minimalism and post-processing.
